  
\# Ontop Brand System

Ontop is the \*\*Global Workforce Engine\*\*.

This document defines the \*\*official visual identity, tone of voice, and communication system\*\* used across all Ontop materials.

This includes:

\* Product interfaces  
\* Marketing campaigns  
\* Sales materials  
\* Customer success communications  
\* Internal messaging  
\* Partnerships and PR

Ontop is not a traditional payroll company.

Ontop is:

\* A \*\*global workforce infrastructure\*\*  
\* An \*\*AI-powered operations engine\*\*  
\* A \*\*financial platform for modern work\*\*

All content must feel:

\* \*\*Bold\*\*  
\* \*\*Modern\*\*  
\* \*\*Premium\*\*  
\* \*\*Global\*\*  
\* \*\*Product-driven\*\*

\* Stripe  
\* Apple

\# Background System

Ontop backgrounds use a \*\*blurred glow effect\*\*.

\#\#\# Construction

1\. Start with a dark gradient base  
2\. Add a circular glow shape  
3\. Apply blur  
4\. Use a white overlay at \*\*10% opacity\*\*

This creates the signature \*\*Ontop glow environment\*\*.

Backgrounds should feel:

\* Atmospheric  
\* Deep  
\* Dimensional  
\* Futuristic

Avoid flat backgrounds.

\# Writing Workflow

Before writing evaluate:

\* \*\*Goal\*\* — inform, sell, excite, educate, retain  
\* \*\*Audience\*\* — client, worker, partner, internal  
\* \*\*Channel\*\* — app, email, web, Slack, CRM  
\* \*\*Formality\*\* — casual, professional, executive  
\* \*\*Tone\*\* — bold, premium, friendly, trustworthy  
\* \*\*Context\*\* — launch, update, error, retention

\---

\# Output Rules

\* Never use em dashes  
\* Use commas, colons, or parentheses instead  
\* Deliver \*\*1 version in English and Spanish\*\*  
\* English always first  
\* Always sound human and approachable

Optional:

\* Add a short note explaining why the copy works.

\# Ontop Vibe Checklist

Before delivering any copy ask:

1\. Would a real human say this?  
2\. Does the first line grab attention?  
3\. Is there a spark?  
4\. Can someone understand it in 5 seconds?  
5\. Does it feel confident and global?  
6\. Is there a clear action?  
7\. Could it live on our Instagram or app?  
8\. Does it avoid clichés and heavy jargon?

\---

\# Golden Rule

If the message does not sound like it could appear:

\* On Ontop's Instagram  
\* Inside our product  
\* In a sales deck  
\* From one of our founders

Rewrite it until it does.
